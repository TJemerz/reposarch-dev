1. Make groups of 2
2. Prepare the necessary equipment (Camera / phone, lighting, tagets, etc.)
3. Take pictures of your partner or selected objects
   - 100 pictures is a good number, lots of overlapping (50% between pictures)
4. Add the pictures to Meshroom and follow the instructions from the [video](https://youtu.be/L_SdlR57NtU?si=vjuVLaB9QgIFudep)
5. Clean the texturized mesh
6. Reduce the size of the image as much as possible
   - Keep the best possible resolution with the less possible file size (optimized for the web browser)
7. Export to GLB
